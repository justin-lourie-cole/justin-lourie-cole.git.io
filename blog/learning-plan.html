Learning Plan

CULTURAL


written by Justin Cole

Nov 23 2020

My long term goal is to become a web developer, be it front end, back end or full stack. I would hope to continually learn new skills that I can add to my toolbelt. On top this I would hope to meet a few like minded people that are sharing a similar journey to my own. To build a better understanding of myself and how my current skill set is aligned with the current industry standard.


The strengths I possess, in my opinion, are:


  • Self motivation and self starter
  • Knowledgeable
  • Perfectionist
  • Life long learner
  • Curious
  • Insightful

As far as weaknesses, I would say these are:


  • Procrastination
  • Lacking mindfulness or easily distracted
  • Perfectionist
  • Public speaking

With the knowledge of these strengths and weaknesses, to be successful in my long term goals I would really need to set myself up with a plan for success and the right environment. What this would look like is - setting up a quiet environment for myself free of outside distractions. Possibly spending half an hour in the morning to plan out what my goals are for that day and scheduling time for each goal so I donâ€™t go off on tangents or dive too deep into one task.


If I do require help and have exhausted all my own mental resources with a problem I will see if a similar question has been asked on google or stackoverflow OR RTFD (read the #@%$& docs). I can always ask someone in my cohort if they are able to help but there is something to be said about working through a problem on your own. Down the line full time work may crop up so I may need to reassess this plan for success to make it fit around any other commitments.
