To start identifying my personal values, probably the easiest inroad is to frame them in a professional setting. In my professional career the values I identify with and value the most are professionalism, hard work and ownership. Having worked with people who lacked these qualities then seeing a shift in their attitude, the impact on the culture and success of the business these values have is clear as day.
